四边形ABCD中，角A是60度，角B和角D都是直角。已知AB:AD=8:7，求BC:CD。<br>

In quadrilateral ABCD, angle A is 60 degrees, and angles B and D are both right angles. Given that AB:AD=8:7, find BC:CD.


### Solution <button>Show / Hide</button>

<solution>
         
  BC : CD = 2 : 3
